About Me

2020-11-26 :: Rahul
The areas of research that I am interested in primarily lie within the realm of computation and the mathematical and statistical methods employed therein. My ongoing research on FRD dynamics in peri-urban mosaics has allowed me to apply some of these advanced concepts to a complex geospatial system and has led to a furthering of my understanding of these theories and how to efficiently apply them. Underlying my work and interests is simply a long-standing love for data and what one can do with contemporarily. Ultimately I would like to well acquaint myself with the data sciences and the applications of more recent theories such as graph and chaos theory as well as the computational power available in the 21st century to further insights derivable from data.⌗
